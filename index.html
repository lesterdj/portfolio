iNervous is a character who represents all those everyday mishaps that anyone can relate to.

His life is a never-ending sequence of comical misadventures: landing on a dog poop while performing a skate trick, receiving a "gift" on his head from a pigeon.

What makes iNervous special is how he handles these situations: with exaggerated bursts of anger that make him shout his frustration to the world.
